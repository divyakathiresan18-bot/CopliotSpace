# Persona Role Matrix

This matrix helps teams quickly see primary responsibilities and primary contacts for common activities.

| Activity / Area | Primary Role | Secondary / Support Roles | Typical Trigger |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Define success metrics | Data Analyst / PM | Developers | Project kickoff / feature design
| Architecture decision | Technical Lead | Developers | Significant change / design review
| Sprint delivery | Delivery Lead | Developers, QA | Sprint planning
| Release coordination | Release Lead | Delivery Lead, QA, Support Lead | Pre-release checklist complete
| Incident response | Support Lead / On-call | Developers, Release Lead | Pager / monitoring alert
| Stakeholder communication | Project Manager / PM | Delivery Lead | Weekly status or incident

Use this matrix to populate project READMEs and onboarding guides.
